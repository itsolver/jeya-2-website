My Story

I am passionate about beautifying living spaces.

Jeya Spencer

Thank you for stopping by. As owner of Jeya Spencer Styling, I am passionate about beautifying living spaces. 

My fascination with beautiful houses and gardens began as a youngster being inspired by my mum and taught how to maintain an immaculate home and garden. As a school kid, I earned money on the weekends cleaning yards, polishing floors and organizing pantries.

Four years ago I built my own house, an opportunity to give my decorating and gardening talent free rein. Making my home beautiful gives me so much satisfaction. Over the years, I've studied and researched interior decorating, and completing the Staging Diva course greatly helped me start my home staging business.

For the past eight years, I have been working with people living with disabilities. I love my job. It is rewarding to know I'm making a difference for the people I support and their loved ones. I want to extend my supportive experience and decorating talents to help homeowners successfully sell their properties.

Selling a property is a complex process. Let me handle the details of home staging/styling to simplify it all to your complete satisfaction. Whether it's readying a house to sell or restyling a room, let my talent work for  you to stage, redesign or reorganize.

Please explore my website and if you have any questions, please contact us.
